Further thoughts for a sequel to "The Arrow of Time":

The dashboard as a Markov Blanket.

My definition: "Consciousness is the awareness of space and time." needs clarifying: Consciousness is really the awareness of Chunked Objects and Events. One is never aware of space and time without objects and events. Space and Time are just convenient names for relations between Objects and Events. So, should Consciousness be better defined as the Chunking of undifferentiated Reality.
We can deliberately choose to look at the outside world in an unchunked way (its an uncomfortable experience, that can't be sustained for long). Trying not to chunk events is the same as trying not to think. Can one be said to be conscious in such a state? One could say that one is then conscious of unchunked sensory input, but that input is effectively being prevented from being used in any further processing, so its literally useless.

Towards a solution (some random thoughts):

  1. Consciousness is the process of chunking objects (=space) and events (=time) out of unchunked reality. According to this definition, the "dashboard" (of experience) is the border between chunked and unchunked reality. What we experience on the dashboard are chunked objects and events. There are no differentiated objects or events in the reality beyond the dashboard, but there is something there that can be chunked.
  2. Physics tells us that entangled particles lose their individuality.
  3. The Apollo Sunburst illusion: Confused perception of the individual blades, leads to a dissolution of their individuality, so they appear to interchange, and the Sunburst appears to rotate, while not rotating. (Look at what I said in Retrospective 2024 again.)
  4. Time does not exist in unperceived reality, but the Universe nevertheless appears to evolve (on this side of the dashboard). How is this like the Sunburst illusion?
  5. Is there a way, inside this paradox, for time-loops to happen (beyond the dashboard) that would allow the bootstrapping of the construction (evolution) of protein hierarchies on this side of the dashboard? See also Kaufmann's thoughts on the evolution of complexity in Link #2 below.
Other thoughts:
  1. Another reason for thinking about "intelligent design" is the "fine tuning" (unlikely state) of the fundamental physical constants. Can my thoughts towards a solution say anything about that? Kaufmann provides some interesting thoughts on this in Link #3 below.
  2. Can the two-slit experiment be explained in terms of unobserved (entangled) particles going through the two slits at the same time? The Schrödinger equation works by describing the probabilities of particular observations, not the underlying (event-less, object-less) physics. "Unobserved particles" is self-contradictory, since a "particle" is a chunked entity, and consciousness (=observation) is the process of chunking. We need to be very careful when using plurals here: "particles" cannot mean many instances of a "particle" here. "particles" actually means an entity in unchunked reality that can be chunked into many (chunked) particles. So such entities must exist (in unchunked reality).
